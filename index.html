Privacy Policy for Carvio - Renter Information

Thank you for using Carvio, the rent-a-car platform. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how we collect, use, and safeguard the information provided by individuals who offer their vehicles for renting (referred to as "renters") through the Carvio application. By utilizing our services, renters agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect:

a. Contact Information:

We collect renter contact information, including phone numbers, for the purpose of facilitating communication between Carvio and renters. This information is used for transactional and operational communication.

b. Vehicle Ownership Verification:

For added trust and transparency within the Carvio community, we may verify the ownership of vehicles by requesting the submission of the Registration Certificate (RC book) or other relevant ownership documents during the registration process. This verification process contributes to ensuring the credibility of the Carvio platform.

We want to emphasize that this verification is specifically related to confirming the ownership details of the vehicles listed on Carvio. Your privacy and sensitive information are handled with the utmost care and are not shared without your explicit consent.

Your data's security is a priority for us, and we implement measures to safeguard it from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

c. Vehicle Information:

We collect comprehensive data about the rented vehicles, including make, model, year, registration details, images, and any additional information provided by renters. This information is used to create detailed vehicle profiles that are displayed on the Carvio platform.

d. Location Information:

Renter provides the location where their vehicle is available for renting. It's important to note that we do not collect real-time or current location data of the renter or their vehicle. The provided location is a static representation for the purpose of helping potential renters find suitable vehicles.

e. Vehicle Images:

Renter provides images of their vehicle to showcase its condition and features on the Carvio platform. These images are displayed to potential renters to aid in their decision-making process.

2. How We Use Renter Information:

a. Booking Management:

Renter information is primarily used to manage vehicle bookings, including but not limited to, communication with renters, coordination of pick-up and drop-off locations, and facilitating the rental process.

b. Profile Display:

Renter's vehicle information and images may be displayed on the Carvio platform to attract potential renters. A well-detailed and visually appealing profile increases the likelihood of successful bookings.

c. Communication:

Renter contact information is used for communication purposes, such as sending booking confirmations, providing updates related to their vehicle, and resolving any issues that may arise during the rental period.

3. Information Sharing:

a. User Visibility:

Renter information, including vehicle details and images, may be visible to users interested in renting a vehicle. This visibility is essential to facilitate a transparent and informed decision-making process for potential renters.

b. Third-Party Services:

We do not share renter information with third-party services for marketing or any other purposes. The information shared on the Carvio platform is solely for the purpose of facilitating vehicle rentals.

4. Security Measures:

a. Data Security:

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect renter information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption of data in transit and at rest.

5. Renter Choices:

a. Access and Modification:

Renters have the right to access and modify their profile information through the Carvio application. This includes updating vehicle details, contact information, and any other relevant information.

b. Opt-out:

Renters can opt-out of receiving promotional communications from Carvio at any time. However, transactional and operational communications related to bookings and rentals may still be sent.

6. Updates to this Privacy Policy:

a. Policy Changes:

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices. Renters will be notified of any significant changes, and the latest version of the policy will be made accessible through the Carvio application.

7. Contact Us:

a. Questions or Concerns:

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact our dedicated support team at wheelsondemandwod@gmail.com. We are here to address your inquiries and ensure a transparent and secure experience on the Carvio platform.

8. Legal Compliance:

a. Jurisdictional Compliance:

Renters are responsible for complying with all relevant laws and regulations related to vehicle renting in their jurisdiction. Carvio operates with the understanding that renters will adhere to legal requirements and obligations.

Thank you for entrusting Carvio with your vehicle rental services. We are committed to safeguarding your privacy and providing a secure and user-friendly experience.
